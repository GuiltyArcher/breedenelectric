Meet the Owners

Breeden Electric Inc. is owned by Rennie & Roberta Breeden, who are a dedicated couple with a strong team of experts that focus on helping people the best way possible with their electrical needs.

Rennie received an education at Elizabeth City State University and Roberson Community College, where he received his diploma in Electrical Installation and Maintenance.

After working for several years at Lanehart Electric Company of Charlotte, He went to business for himself in 2005. With Roberta's education from Anson Community College and experience as a former business owner, she performs the function of Project Coordinator for Breeden Electric Inc.

Our goal is to professionally and accurately install wiring, lighting, and receptacles according to industry standards. The overall mission for Breeden Electric Inc. is to bring light into others' lives through quality service and excellent electrical installations.
